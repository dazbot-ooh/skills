# Majority Orange

Maximum oOh! brand energy. Bold orange is the dominant visual throughout. Used for the highest-impact brand moments.

**Reference PDF:** `themes/majority-orange.pdf`

## Best Used For

Cover slides, section breakers, events, pull-up banners, bespoke/campaign presentations, "Unmissable" brand moments, award submissions.

| Slide type              | Background              | Text/accent colour |

| Brand statement         | oOh! Orange (full slide)| Enormous white CG Regular (80–100pt) — purely typographic |
| Statement on white      | Standard White / near-white | Large oOh! Orange CG Bold (~36pt) |
| Achievement / "#1" block| oOh! Orange             | White text          |
| Section divider         | oOh! Orange             | White "02." CG Bold |
| Stats / big numbers     | White                   | oOh! Orange CG Bold 48–72pt; Twilight Purple secondary panel |
| Key Statement           | Moonlight Purple        | White CG Bold 36pt  |
| Full-bleed photo        | Photography             | oOh! logo bottom-right white |
| End slide               | oOh! branded            | oOh! logo           |

## Rules

- Orange is the dominant background for most non-content slides — cover, statement slides, section dividers
- Big numbers/stats remain on white background for maximum contrast and legibility
- Key Statement slide remains Moonlight Purple (consistent across all themes)
- Twilight Purple used for secondary stat panels on dashboard/data slides
- oOh! logo always present bottom-right on every slide
- All photography full-bleed — no borders or cropped frames
- Sentence case for all headings — no ALL CAPS, no title case
- No decorative lines under titles
