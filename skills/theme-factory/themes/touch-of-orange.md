# Touch of Orange

The default oOh!media theme. White-dominant with oOh! Orange used selectively for accents, statistics, and headings. The most versatile and widely used oOh! deck style.

**Reference PDF:** `themes/touch-of-orange.pdf`

## Best Used For

Corporate reports, text-heavy decks, detailed proposals, investor materials, data-driven presentations.

## Colour Palette

| Role                        | Colour           | HEX       |
|-----------------------------|------------------|-----------|
| Primary accent / headings   | oOh! Orange      | `#FF5F2C` |
| Most slide backgrounds      | Standard White   | `#FFFFFF` |
| Section dividers / subtle bg| Urban Grey       | `#F2F2F2` |
| Key Statement slide bg      | Moonlight Purple | `#14172A` |
| Text on dark slides         | Standard White   | `#FFFFFF` |
| Body copy on white          | Moonlight Purple | `#14172A` |
| Data viz (secondary)        | Twilight Purple  | `#6A2AE1` |

## Typography

- **Font:** Century Gothic only — no exceptions
- **CSS reference:** `font-family: 'Century Gothic', CenturyGothic, AppleGothic, sans-serif`

| Element              | Font            | Size    |
|----------------------|-----------------|---------|
| Headings             | CG Regular      | 24pt    |
| Small headings       | CG Regular      | 16pt    |
| Body copy            | CG Regular      | 12pt    |
| Section number       | CG Regular/Bold | 60pt    |
| Section title        | CG Regular/Bold | 44pt    |
| Key statement        | CG Regular/Bold | 36pt    |
| Big stats/metrics    | CG Bold         | 48–72pt |
| Footnotes            | CG Regular      | min 9pt |

## Slide Type Guidance

| Slide type        | Background       | Text/accent colour |
|-------------------|------------------|--------------------|
| Cover             | oOh! Orange      | White              |
| Content / body    | White            | Moonlight Purple body; oOh! Orange headings |
| Stats / metrics   | White            | oOh! Orange (large numbers CG Bold 48–72pt) |
| Key Statement     | Moonlight Purple | White CG Bold 36pt |
| Section breaker   | Moonlight Purple | White "02." 60pt; oOh! Orange section title |
| Full-bleed photo  | Photography      | oOh! logo bottom-right white |
| End slide         | oOh! branded     | oOh! logo          |

## Rules

- Orange appears as accents and typography only — no full-orange panels except the cover
- Twilight Purple only appears in data viz and infographic slides
- oOh! logo always present bottom-right on every slide
- All photography full-bleed — no borders or cropped frames
- Sentence case for all headings — no ALL CAPS, no title case
- No decorative lines under titles
