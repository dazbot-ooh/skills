# Mid-weight Orange

A balanced oOh!media theme with more orange panels alongside white content slides. More brand energy than Touch of Orange, without going full bespoke.

**Reference PDF:** `themes/mid-weight-orange.pdf`

## Best Used For

Standard brand presentations, pitches, agency decks, proposals, partner presentations.

## Colour Palette

| Role                             | Colour           | HEX       |
|----------------------------------|------------------|-----------|
| Primary accent / orange panels   | oOh! Orange      | `#FF5F2C` |
| Content slide backgrounds        | Standard White   | `#FFFFFF` |
| Section dividers / subtle bg     | Urban Grey       | `#F2F2F2` |
| Key Statement slide bg           | Moonlight Purple | `#14172A` |
| Section divider slide bg         | oOh! Orange      | `#FF5F2C` |
| Text on dark / orange slides     | Standard White   | `#FFFFFF` |
| Body copy on white               | Moonlight Purple | `#14172A` |
| Secondary stat accent            | Twilight Purple  | `#6A2AE1` |

## Typography

- **Font:** Century Gothic only — no exceptions
- **CSS reference:** `font-family: 'Century Gothic', CenturyGothic, AppleGothic, sans-serif`

| Element              | Font            | Size    |
|----------------------|-----------------|---------|
| Headings             | CG Regular      | 24pt    |
| Small headings       | CG Regular      | 16pt    |
| Body copy            | CG Regular      | 12pt    |
| Section number       | CG Regular/Bold | 60pt    |
| Section title        | CG Regular/Bold | 44pt    |
| Key statement        | CG Regular/Bold | 36pt    |
| Big stats/metrics    | CG Bold         | 48–72pt |
| Footnotes            | CG Regular      | min 9pt |

## Slide Type Guidance

| Slide type              | Background            | Text/accent colour |
|-------------------------|-----------------------|--------------------|
| Cover                   | oOh! Orange           | White              |
| Content / body          | White                 | Moonlight Purple body; oOh! Orange subheadings CG Bold |
| Hero statement          | Full-bleed photo + left-third orange overlay | White text on orange panel |
| Stats / metrics         | White                 | oOh! Orange (large numbers CG Bold 48–72pt); Twilight Purple secondary panel |
| Key Statement           | Moonlight Purple      | White CG Bold 36pt |
| Section divider         | oOh! Orange (full slide) | White "02. Section" CG Bold |
| Dashboard               | White                 | Orange primary metrics; Twilight Purple secondary metrics |
| Full-bleed photo        | Photography           | oOh! logo bottom-right white |
| End slide               | oOh! branded          | oOh! logo          |

## Rules

- More orange panels than Touch of Orange — section dividers and hero slides use full orange
- Twilight Purple used as a secondary stat accent on dashboard/data slides
- Key Statement slide remains Moonlight Purple (same as Touch of Orange)
- oOh! logo always present bottom-right on every slide
- All photography full-bleed — no borders or cropped frames
- Sentence case for all headings — no ALL CAPS, no title case
- No decorative lines under titles
